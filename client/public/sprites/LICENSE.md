## Streets of Fight (Brawler Girl + Enemy Punk strips)

- **Pack:** Streets of Fight  
- **Author:** Luis Zuno (**Ansimuz**)  
- **Source:** [itch.io — Streets of Fight](https://ansimuz.itch.io/streets-of-fight)  
- **Shipped copy in repo:** `client/public/sprites/Streets of Fight files/` (includes `public-license.pdf` by Luis Zuno)  
- **How we use it:** Horizontal strips normalized into `client/public/sprites/cyber_brawler/` (female brawler) and `client/public/sprites/neon_punk/` (punk enemy), referenced by `manifest.json`.

The itch.io listing states the pack is ready for personal or commercial use; **follow the terms in `public-license.pdf`** for redistribution/credits if they differ.
